EN' DECORE is an affable name that evolved due to the meticulous effort developed by a visionary approach in extending the possibilities of manufacturing a vibrant and cozy home for you. We have been adding value to the business we are engaged in; through our sheer business acumen, penchant towards innovation & technology, versatility to upgrade ourselves, and above all - our stringent commitment to deliver only the best. .

To become an indisputable name in offering quality, reliable products that match the global standards and strive towards enhancing the credentials with a visionary approach to deliver products par excellence EN' DECORE offers an option of customization (you can customize on your own or take the recommendation of our interior designers). .

To promote a constant and continuous effort in understanding the market requirements through a regular watch on the burgeoning demands, thereby providing a wide range of products that speak volumes about engineering excellence and elegance. EN' DECORE offers you a wide range of products from modern to vintage styles adding aesthetics to your home. .

We focus on research and development aligned with the addition of innovatively designed new products. We are in a continuous process of designing and manufacturing a wide range of modern furniture that stands in line with ever-evolving trends and needs. With an extensive array of new products, we are striving to explore new horizons. We came up with an AR app for this purpose. .

Augmented Reality is all about overlaying virtual animated objects onto real-world surroundings. Users with the help of mobile devices can experience realistic visualization anytime and anywhere. The AR applications can help users virtually try on furniture to their smartphone with personalized instructions. AR can help to improve the retention rate of users by superimposing products with real-life environments. Help to reduce pre-purchase indecision while cutting the rate of product return post-purchase. AR also enables to easily personalize the product offering tailored promotions based on personal preferences. .
