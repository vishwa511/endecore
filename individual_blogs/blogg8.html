DREAM HOME: A MODERN ENGLISH FARMHOUSE

~BHUVAN JHAMB



Today’s dream home is so pretty. It’s a modern English farmhouse that perfectly balances crisp clean lines and soft traditional textures. You’re going to love both the architecture and the interior design. Clean lines pair with warm colours and textures in the kitchen. The space feels crisp and modern but also has a charming old-world warmth. Black frame windows have been a major trend over the past few years, but one that has been around for a long time and one we think will prove timeless. Black chairs mimic the dark windows, while a mix of textiles adds warmth. So pretty. The living space is soft and airy with a beautiful mix of patterns. That coffee table is a beautiful whitewash that is super versatile no matter your style. The black and white foundations in the mud and laundry rooms create a clean classic look. Both spaces are styled to feel inviting, which is important in making the chores done in these spaces more enjoyable. The hallway alone between the bedroom and bathroom is dreamy. Rugs should be layered over the carpet. Even in a soft carpeted space, area rugs add beautiful colour, pattern, and an extra luxe look. The exterior of the dream home uses white, black, and wood. The colour combination is on-trend, but a look to love for its clean timeless appeal. The black-on-black garages feel crisp and modern but are softened with the white and wood walk-through. It’s an exterior to love. This dream house will make your heart flutter and dance in the breeze. Good design is understandable to virtually everybody. You never have to ask why.
