PLANNING YOUR “WOW MOMENT”

~SHRUTI BHATEJA



Okay, back to myths. The biggest myth that I hear over and over again maybe one that you believe yourself – that luxe-looking interior design has to cost a fortune. Now listen, I wasn’t exactly an A+ student, but when it comes to the home, I will sit down with my big stack of magazines, scissors, and a cup of coffee (or a glass of wine if it’s after 5 PM) and get to work! By taking this time to just look and play without any pressure, clients can start to get a better sense of what they’re drawn to. I’ll then tell them to find a room in one of the magazines that are similarly laid out to the room they’re looking to design. Now look closely. Where is the furniture placed? What about the lighting? What about it made you stop at this page in the first place? Are you drawn to a colour, a texture, or maybe just a specific shape? So, if you’ve been waiting for a sign to turn your home into a space that you love, you’re in luck. EN' DECORE is the perfect platform for all those out there who wanna turn their homes into vibrant places. We’re going to make it chic; we’re going to make it affordable, and we’re going to make it “wow” – because if there’s one thing I cannot stand, it’s a basic you-know-what! It accommodates the latest technology of AR adding a whole new dimension to the world of interior designing. Finally, you don't need to struggle to find ideas, replicate something you’ve seen online and then move things around to make it your own. Oh, and this course is also for someone who wants to feel like Marie Antoinette walking through The Louvre on the way to eat cake in bed. So, what are you waiting for you, a degree from Kingston college of art?
